WELCOME TO OUR RESEARCH GROUP

This group is led by Professor.Yulei Sui, an Associate Professor at School of Computer Science, Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology, University of Technology Sydney (UTS). Our research focuses on building fundamental static analysis and verification techniques to improve the reliability and security of modern software systems.
