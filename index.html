WELCOME TO OUR RESEARCH GROUP

This group is led by Professor.Yulei Sui, an Associate Professor at School of Computer Science, Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology (FEIT), University of Technology Sydney (UTS).

Our research focuses on building Fundamental Static Analysis and Verification Techniques to improve the Reliability and Security of modern software systems.

OUR INTEREST
Secure Software
Engineering

From software development and vulnerability attack, complete the architecture of software security.

Program
Analysis

Taking entire production as the research object, analyzes redundancy, duplication or unreasonable phenomenon, and develops improvement plans.

Machine
Learning

Study how computers simulate human learning behavior to acquire new knowledge and improve their own performance.
